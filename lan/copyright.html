Copyright meets design:
When designing, you are in the business of creating intellectual property (IP). Your IP is a valuable business
asset and knowing how to protect it can be central to your business success. Even as a student, it is something
that you need to take into consideration as your designs become a part of your practice and learning.

Below are some links to resources where you can learn about your intellectual property to protect your hard
work, integrity and creativity.
